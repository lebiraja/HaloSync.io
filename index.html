Key Features

Our AR glasses provide instant information about the products you’re browsing. Whether it’s online or in-store, you’ll see price comparisons, reviews, and product availability.

Personalized Recommendations

AI-driven recommendations tailored to your preferences for a more curated shopping experience. Discover the products you love before you even search for them.

Virtually place products in your space before you buy them. Preview furniture, fashion, or home decor through augmented reality, eliminating guesswork from your shopping experience.

Control your shopping journey without lifting a finger. Browse, select, and compare products using simple hand gestures, making the experience smooth and intuitive.

Finalizing your purchase has never been easier. Our integrated payment system allows you to complete transactions in seconds, right through the glasses interface.
